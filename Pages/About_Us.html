Frequently asked questions

History of our website
This website was created during the Covid pandemic(25.3.2020) to raise awareness about people with ADHD and Autism especially during the pandemic since people were free and have a bunch of time to read articles .
Why do we make this website?
We established this website because the majority of people in our community and country are unaware of ADHD and Autism, which means they do not know how to treat or care for people with these conditions. Our website will also help to develop an inclusive environment (by allowing people to express themselves openly), increase acceptance, and promote early intervention and support. In summary, we built this website to promote awareness of people with ADHD and Autism and how we can help them.
What benefits we want from our website?
We want people in our country and around us to understand more about autism and ADHD. We believe that after visiting our website, people will have a better understanding of how to handle people with ADHD and autism, as well as the fact that they are human and should not be made fun of. We thought that some people might want to know if they have autism/ADHD, so we decided to provide some quizzes to determine whether a person has autism or ADHD.
Our future plans and goals
We intend to update our website with additional information, interviews, campaigns, posters, and films. We also aim to raise awareness and provide information about people with ADHD and autism, as they are often bullied and cyberbullied because of their disorders and disabilities. We also want to improve the look of our website by adding additional animations, transitions, images, and vibrant designs.
